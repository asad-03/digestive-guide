Sunnah Digestive

Each prayer time = eating/drinking cue

Fajr (Pre-dawn)

🍴 Food: 1-3 khajoor + honey water
💧 Drink: Shahad pani (lukewarm)
💡 Eat slowly; chew 30+ times

After Fajr (1-2 hrs later)

🍴 Food: Dahi + khajoor OR Jau ka daliya
💧 Drink: Sips of water
💡 Barley is cooling & digestive

Mid-morning

🍴 Food: Rest (no food)
💧 Drink: Water only (sips)
💡 Let Fajr meal digest

Dhuhr (Noon)

🍴 Food: Kheera + Chawal + Dahi
💧 Drink: Water (after 30 mins)
💡 Light lunch. Eat 1/3 full

Post-Dhuhr (2-3 hrs)

🍴 Food: Rest
💧 Drink: Adrak chai OR Podina pani
💡 Ginger aids digestion; mint soothes

Asr (Late afternoon)

🍴 Food: 2-3 khajoor
💧 Drink: Saunf pani (fennel water)
💡 KEY: Reduces gas & bloating

Maghrib (Sunset)

🍴 Food: Date + small cup milk
💧 Drink: Milk or water
💡 Aids nutrient absorption

After Maghrib (1-2 hrs)

🍴 Food: MAIN MEAL: Daal + Roti + Sabzi
💧 Drink: Small sips of water
💡 Largest meal of day. Eat slowly!

Isha (Night)

🍴 Food: Herbal tea only
💧 Drink: Podina chai (mint tea)
💡 Aids digestion & sleep

Before Sleep (3-4 hrs)

🍴 Food: Nothing
💧 Drink: Water only if thirsty
💡 Empty stomach = better digestion

• Doodhwala: 6-8am daily

Khajoor (Dates)

📍 Where: Urdu Bazaar, Anarkali
💰 Cost: 200-300/kg
💡 Tip: Buy in bulk June-August

Shahad (Fresh Honey)

📍 Where: Health shops, Packages Mall
💰 Cost: 500-800/bottle
💡 Tip: Direct from Sialkot keepers

Jau (Barley)

📍 Where: Kisan Bazaar, grain shops
💰 Cost: 80-120/kg
💡 Tip: Buy unpolished

Fresh Dahi (Yogurt)

📍 Where: Neighborhood doodhwala
💰 Cost: 100-150/liter
💡 Tip: Order daily delivery

Podina (Fresh Mint)

📍 Where: Sabzi Mandi
💰 Cost: 20-40/bunch
💡 Tip: Fresh Wed-Sat

Adrak (Fresh Ginger)

📍 Where: Sabzi Mandi
💰 Cost: 50-80/kg
💡 Tip: Available year-round

Masoor Daal

📍 Where: Dal shops, Kisan Bazaar
💰 Cost: 150-200/kg
💡 Tip: Buy in bulk (25kg bags)
